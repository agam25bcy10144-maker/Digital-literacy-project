🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students 
   
    ✅ General Safety Tips
1)Use strong, unique passwords for every account and enable 2-factor authentication (2FA)
2)Do not click on unknown links or download files from untrusted sources
3)Always verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news
4)Keep your apps, antivirus, and system updated regularly
5)Be careful while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into sensitive accounts
6)Adjust privacy settings on social media and limit personal information sharing
     
     
     💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips
1)Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
2)Do not approve unknown payment requests or “collect requests” on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe
3)Double-check the receiver’s name before sending money
  
  
  🚨 Report Cybercrime
1)Report incidents on National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
2)Call Helpline: 1930 immediately in case of financial fraud
