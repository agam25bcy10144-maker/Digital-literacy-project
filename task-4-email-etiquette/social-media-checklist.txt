PART-B


✅ Do’s
1)Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings
2)Share accurate and verified information
3)Be respectful and polite in your posts and comments
4)Think before posting anything online
5)Maintain a professional profile and image
 
 ❌ Don’ts
1)Don’t share personal or sensitive information
2)Don’t spread fake news or rumors
3)Don’t post offensive or harmful content
4)Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
5)Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown messages
